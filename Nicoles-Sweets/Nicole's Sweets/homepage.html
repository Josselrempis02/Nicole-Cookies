Our story

Our story began in 2019, when our founder, Nicole Cillo started reselling a homemade chocolates and candies. Her treats were so delicious that soon everyone was asking for more. As demand grew, Nicole decided to enter on a business and opened Nicole's Sweetin a small storefront in downtown. She began by selling her chocolates, gummies, and candies, and eventually expanded to include other treats like cookies,marshmallow and more. Over the years,Nicole's Sweet has become a beloved institution in the community.
